The Goal
Lately, I’ve been reading almost exclusively on my e-reader. While I love the convenience, I’ve really started to miss the presence of physical books. There’s a specific satisfaction in looking at a bookshelf and seeing your history as a reader right in front of you.

With this project, I want to bridge that gap. I'm building a space to not only track what I’ve read but to create a virtual 3D library that captures the feeling of standing in a real room full of books.

To make it even more personal, I’ll be modeling the 3D assets myself.

This is just the beginning—I'll be updating this README as the library grows!
